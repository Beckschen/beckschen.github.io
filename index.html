Jie-Neng Chen

I am a second-year Computer Science Ph.D. student at Johns Hopkins University, where I am fortunate to be advised by Bloomberg Distinguished Professor Alan Yuille.

Previously I worked as research intern at Bytedance, PingAn, and SenseTime. I did my undergraduate at Tongji Uni.

My research interests lie on vision Transformer, image segmentation and medical AI.
